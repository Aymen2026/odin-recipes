Chakchouka's Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Half a big piece of onion
  • 2 Green bell peppers
  • Oil(any type)
  • 2 Medium sized tomatoes
  • Red skinny pepper
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Put a spoonful of oil on a pan on low heat.
  2. Cut the half piece of onion into little cubes and put them on the pan.
  3. After the onion turns yellow, cut the bell peppers and add them.
  4. Cut the tomatoes into little cubes and add them.
  5. Grate the garlic and red pepper and add them to the pan.
  6. Mix and stir until cooked.
  7. Crack the eggs and add them
