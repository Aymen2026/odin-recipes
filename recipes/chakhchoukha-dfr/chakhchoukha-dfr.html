Chakhchoukhat Dfar's Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chakhchoukha
  • Butter
  • A piece of meat(any kind but preferably chicken)
  • 2 Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Hot sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Grated Tomato

Steps:

  1. In a pot, we put some better and slices of onion and grated garlic to fry.
  2. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Add hummus, meat and tomato paste.
  4. Add hot water until it becomes like a soup.
  5. On the other hand, steam the chakhchoukha with normal water.
  6. Steam it again but on the soup instead.

Enjoy;)!
